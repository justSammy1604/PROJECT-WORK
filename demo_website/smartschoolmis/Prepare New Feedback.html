Instructions :

  • Step 1 : Prepare new feedback for service/event/product etc. Here you will get list of login passwords for students.
  • Step 2 : Add questions for which you want to know responses of students.
  • Step 3 : Start or Stop feedback when you want to allow or stop students for login.
  • Step 4 : Manage details of feedback. You can delete or update information of registered feedbacks.
  • Step 5 : Manage institute profile. You can delete or update information of your institute.
  • Step 6 : Click on Take Feedback. Here you will get the login window for students in new tab.
  • Step 7 : Feedback result is generated automatically after the end of a feedback taking process. Here you can print or export results.
